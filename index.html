A College Resource Booking System is designed to manage and allocate shared facilities like classrooms, labs, seminar halls, sports grounds, and auditoriums in an efficient and organized manner. Its main use is to streamline the booking process, reduce scheduling conflicts, and ensure fair access to resources for students, faculty, and staff. Through the system, users can check availability, request bookings, and receive confirmations—all in real-time. The interaction is simple and user-friendly: a student or teacher logs into the system using their college ID, selects the required resource, picks a date and time, and submits a request. Administrators can then review, approve, or reject the request based on availability and rules. Users receive notifications about the status of their bookings and can also modify or cancel them if needed. This level of interaction not only saves time but also promotes transparency and accountability. By making the process digital, the system enhances coordination between departments, improves resource utilization, and helps in organizing academic and extracurricular events smoothly.
